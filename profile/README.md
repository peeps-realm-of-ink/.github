<h2>Purpose of Realm of Ink</h2>
<p><strong>Realm of Ink</strong> is an ink-style action roguelite developed by <strong>Leap Studio</strong> (with Maple Leaf Studio) and published by <strong>663 Games</strong>. It immerses the player in a stylized Eastern-fantasy world where the main heroine, Red the Swordswoman, gradually uncovers that she and all inhabitants are fictional characters within a book - and must fight against preordained fate. The game aims to blend action, RPG and roguelite elements with distinctive visual art and deep customisation systems.
